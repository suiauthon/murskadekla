LONDON DRY GIN 42% ABV

Murska Dekla London dry gin is the first gin in the family named after a mystic creature from Međimurje, a fairy, a spirit of water and goddess of nature. Like its namesake fairy, our gin carries the secrets of nature, the secret recipe that distills a wealth of herbs.

In the creation of this drink, we took great care in picking ingredients of local and organic origin. Only the best of nature’s gifts was extracted through traditional distillation in copper pot stills. Finally, we were rewarded with a first-class London dry of supreme taste. Enjoy it mixed with tonic water, in one of our exclusive cocktails, or simply on rocks.
